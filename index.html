Our Origin Story - Founded in 2012

Breaking the Cycle of Poverty Through Action

Founded in 2012, Naropil Children Foundation emerged from a deep concern for the high number of children unable to access basic education and healthcare in rural Kenya. We believe poverty should not dictate a child's potential or future.

Our approach is holistic; we don't just pay fees. We walk the journey with each child, offering the psychological, emotional, and social support needed to heal from trauma and build lasting confidence.

Our Vision

A world where every vulnerable child has equal access to opportunities.

Our Mission

To sustainably improve lives through education, health, and mentorship.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every decision we make.

Transparency

We maintain 100% financial accountability to our donors, partners, and the communities we serve.

Compassion

Empathy drives our work. We treat every child with the dignity and love they deserve.

Sustainability

We don't just offer handouts; we empower communities with skills for long-term independence.

What We Do

Our Core Programs

Targeted, sustainable interventions designed to address the unique and complex challenges faced by vulnerable families in our communities.

Education

Comprehensive scholarships covering primary, secondary, and tertiary education, including uniforms, books, and boarding fees for total orphans.

Health & Nutrition

Ensuring every child has access to basic medical care, registering families for NHIF, and running school feeding programs to eradicate malnutrition.

Mentorship

Providing psychological counseling, life-skills training, and career guidance during school holidays to help youth navigate life's challenges safely.

Livelihoods

Empowering guardians and older youth with vocational skills, financial literacy, and micro-business grants to foster household independence.
