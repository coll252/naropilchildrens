Our Story

Breaking the Cycle of Poverty

Naropil Children Foundation emerged from a deep concern for the high number of children unable to access basic education and healthcare. We believe poverty should not dictate a child's potential.

Our approach is holistic; we don't just pay fees. We walk the journey with each child, offering psychological, emotional, and social support needed to heal from trauma and build confidence.

Our Core Programs

Targeted interventions designed to address the unique challenges faced by vulnerable families.

Education

Scholarships for primary, secondary, and tertiary education for orphaned children.

Health

Ensuring access to medical care, NHIF coverage, and nutritious meals.

Mentorship

Guidance, emotional support, and career counseling to navigate challenges.

Livelihoods

Empowering guardians with vocational skills and micro-grants.
